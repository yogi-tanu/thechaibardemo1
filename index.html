Why The Chai Bar

We have a mission to capture the hearts of tea lovers everywhere. And we’re not just talking about the flavour, either! We want you to know that you are valuable, and we want you to know that you deserve to be celebrated for your love of tea. That’s why we created The Chai Bar.

01. AUTHENTICITY:
We take authenticity seriously. When you’re ready to taste the difference, be sure to try our blends. You’ll be greeted by a rich aroma and gorgeous colors that make you feel like you’re at a five-star restaurant.

02. A DREAM TO EMPOWER MILLIONS:
At The Chai Bar, we believe in creating value through our business. We make sure to maintain a fun and joyous environment for all our employees, and we’re dedicated to helping them grow with each other.
